2021-04-10

11 November 2014To understand the true quality of people, you must look into their minds, and examine their pursuits and aversions. It is not death that a man should fear, but he should fear never beginning to live. You have power over your mind - not outside events. Realize this,and you will find strength When you arise in the morning, think of what a precious privilege it is to be alive - to breathe, to think, to enjoy,to love.
